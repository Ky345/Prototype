Johnson Landscape Lighting

Design

As exterior lighting designers, this client's work is best appreciated at night. With this in mind, I created a design that used colors inspired by the night sky and water being illuminated by ambient light.

Parallax was omitted save for certain areas of the site to allow the large image panes to be displayed unobstructed by content panels. This puts the client's portfolio on display immediately and incorporates it directly into the design.
